Welcome to Life-Line Hospital

Lifeline multispeciality hospital - that provides a wide spectrum of medical services as well as advanced surgical techniques. it is one of the best multi-speciality hospitals in Pune.it has emerged as the prime provider of high quality professional medical care. Lifeline multispeciality Hospital is a state-of-the-art medical facility with a highly skilled and experienced team of doctors. The nurses and other support staff at the hospital are professionally trained. For its patients, the name lifeline multispeciality Hospital is synonymous with best patient care. The hospital has 22 medical beds along with 5 ICU Beds with Ventilator,pre and post operating rooms with gas lines, monitoring equipment, and defibrillator. Lifeline multispeciality Hospitals Personalised Care. Speedy Recovery. As a speciality hospital, Lifeline multispeciality Hospital offers expert and quality healthcare, with all the benefits of a large hospital in a friendly and more accessible facility.

We focus on continuous quality improvement to provide patients a safe environment to recieve highest quality care
